Apollonia festival of arts - sozopol

The Apollonia Festival of Arts will take place on the 28th of August to the 5 of September in the seaside town of Sozopol. Sozopol is a wonderful place for such a cultural celebration, due to its picturesque Old Town neighborhood and numerous historical landmarks. The 9 day festival will present a plethora of musical and theatrical acts, as well as various art galleries exhibitions, dancers, architects and writers. There are 39 different events on the program, featuring renowned Bulgarian and international performers and artists.

The festival was founded by Professor Dimo Dimov, and first took place in 1984. Since then, it has been rising in popularity, with thousands of people from all over the world coming together to celebrate culture and art as a medium of unification. The festival has been sponsored by the Apollonia art foundation since 1991.
